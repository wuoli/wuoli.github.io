Creative Science

In my spare time, I combine video production with my academic interests in math, biology, and dentistry to make science more accessible to a wider audience. I also enjoy creating art through math—click on the math section for a math-inspired showcase of my mom.

Biology and Stop-Motion

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I became fascinated by how viruses evolve to infect people and how we develop strategies to fight back. Frustrated by misinformation and the lack of accessible explanations about vaccines, I co-founded EzBio Club with friends to present complex biological concepts in an engaging way—what better way to explain science than through stop-motion animation? This project was a turning point for me, shifting my identity from a “computer science girl” to someone who fully embraced biology.

Dentistry and Videos

When my dentist, Dr. Jordan Galpin, moved to a new practice, he needed better marketing. While social media posts weren't generating much engagement, I saw an opportunity to create bilingual educational videos (English & Mandarin) to showcase his expertise and reach a wider audience.

Note: All reels in this account were filmed by me.
